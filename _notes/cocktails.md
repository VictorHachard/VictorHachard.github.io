## Old Fashioned

**Ingredients**
- 50 ml Bourbon or Rye Whiskey  
- 1 Sugar Cube  
- Few Dashes Angostura Bitters  
- Few Dashes Plain Water  

**Instructions**  
1. Place the sugar cube in an old fashioned glass and saturate with bitters.  
2. Add a few dashes of plain water and muddle until dissolved.  
3. Fill the glass with ice cubes and add bourbon or whiskey.  
4. Stir gently.  

## Sweet as the Punch

**Ingredients**
- 4 cl Bacardi Carta Blanca  
- 2 cl Trois Rivières Ambré Agricole  
- 10 cl Fresh Tropical Juice  
- 2 cl Fresh Lime Juice  
- 1 cl Cane Syrup (Guillot)  
- 0.5 cl Absinthe (≈ 50°, just a dash)   
- 4 fresh sage leaves (2 in shaker, 2 as garnish)  

**Instructions**  
1. Pour into the shaker: Bacardi, Trois Rivières, Granini juice, lime juice, cane syrup and absinthe.  
2. Add 2 sage leaves and gently press with a muddler to release the essential oils.  
3. Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for 12–15 seconds.  
4. Strain into a large tumbler filled with ice cubes.  
5. Garnish with 2 sage leaves, slapped between your hands to intensify their aroma.  

## Victor’s Navy Grog

**Ingredients**
- 4 cl Bacardi Carta Blanca 
- 2 cl Trois Rivières  
- 4 cl Fresh Grapefruit Juice  
- 2 cl Fresh Lime Juice  
- 2 cl Honey Mix (1:1 honey and water)  
- 1 cl Falernum  
- 0.5 cl Green Chartreuse

**Method**  
1. Shake all ingredients with crushed ice for 8–10 seconds.  
2. Pour unstrained into an old fashioned glass.  
3. Top with a dome of crushed ice.  

**Garnish**  
- Mint bouquet  
- Grapefruit zest  

## Old Maid

**Ingredients**
- 5 cl Gin  
- 2 cl Fresh Lime Juice  
- 1.5 cl Simple Syrup  
- 4 Mint Leaves  
- 2 Cucumber Slices  

**Instructions**  
1. In a shaker, combine gin, lime juice, simple syrup, mint leaves, and cucumber slices.  
2. Add ice and shake vigorously.  
3. Strain into an old fashioned glass filled with ice.  
4. Garnish with a mint leaf and a cucumber slice.  

## Suffering Bastard

**Ingredients**

* 30 ml Gin
* 30 ml Brandy (or Bourbon or Cognac)
* 20 ml Fresh Lime Juice
* 10 ml Simple Syrup
* 2 Dashes Angostura Bitters
* Ginger Beer (to top)

**Instructions**

1. Shake gin, brandy, lime juice, simple syrup, and bitters with ice.
2. Strain into a tall glass filled with ice cubes.
3. Top with ginger beer and stir gently.
4. Garnish with a mint sprig and a lime wheel.
