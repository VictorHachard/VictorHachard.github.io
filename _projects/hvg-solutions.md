[HVG Solutions](https://hvg-solutions.com) is a Belgian software studio I co-founded and run with another developer. You work directly with the two developers who design and build your software, no project manager and no outsourcing, just the people writing your code.

We do end-to-end custom development and ERP/IT products:

- **ERP & Odoo** - custom Odoo modules, integrations and full ERP implementations for SMEs and multi-site networks.
- **Custom development** - software built around your processes, from idea to production.
- **Automation & integrations** - n8n workflows and APIs that connect your tools, remove manual work and reduce errors.
- **Websites & web platforms** - corporate sites, e-commerce and web platforms that scale with your traffic.

We also design and maintain our own products, including [Solaryx]({{site.baseurl}}/projects/solaryx/), a tanning-salon and wellness management platform, as well as flashboard for tattoo shops and addrly, a Belgian address and company data API.

## What I Learned

- Running a software studio end-to-end
- Odoo module development and full ERP implementations
- Building and maintaining SaaS products
- Workflow automation with n8n and REST APIs
- Working directly with clients from idea to production
